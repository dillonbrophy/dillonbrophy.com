01 About

The ear behind
the records.

Dillon Brophy is a GRAMMY-nominated engineer and producer based out of New York, operating at the highest level of modern music. A graduate of SAE Institute, Brophy has spent over a decade refining his craft across music recording, mixing, mastering, immersive audio, and production.

As one half of a powerhouse partnership with Jess Jackson — co-owner of Antares Audio Technologies (Auto-Tune) — Brophy has engineered and produced records that have topped charts, earned platinum certifications, and been recognized by the Recording Academy.

His co-production on GloRilla's "TGIF" earned TIME Magazine's #1 Song of 2024, went 2x Platinum, hit #1 on R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay, and secured a GRAMMY nomination for Best Rap Song at the 2026 ceremony.
